About Royal Star

Leading Egypt's Logistics Revolution

Founded in 2018, Royal Star has rapidly emerged as a premier provider of land freight and logistics solutions in Egypt. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as the preferred choice for businesses across various industries.

In 2024 alone, we successfully completed 4,260 land freight trips, demonstrating our capacity and reliability. Our strategic focus on key ports includes Alexandria (3,400 trips), Damietta (600 trips), and Sokhna (260 trips), ensuring comprehensive coverage across Egypt's major logistics hubs.

Our mission is to ensure seamless cargo movement across Egypt, enhancing the logistics landscape through professionalism, customer-centricity, and continuous innovation. We envision leading Egypt's inland transportation sector by delivering innovative, secure, and sustainable logistics solutions.

Our Services

Container Transport

Specialized transport solutions for dry, reefer, and flat rack containers. Our advanced tracking systems and security protocols ensure your goods arrive safely and on schedule.

Heavy Cargo & Oversized Equipment

Expert handling of oversized equipment and heavy machinery with specialized equipment and highly experienced personnel. We manage complex logistics for your most challenging shipments.

Low Bed Trucking

Specialized low bed trailers for transporting equipment that requires low ground clearance loading. Ideal for construction machinery, industrial equipment, and other specialized cargo.

Inland Freight Operations

Comprehensive inland freight operations serving all Egyptian ports and major industrial zones. From Alexandria to Sokhna, Damietta to Port Said, we've got you covered.

Warehousing & Storage Solutions

Secure, modern storage facilities with advanced inventory management systems. Our warehouses provide seamless integration with your supply chain operations.

24/7 Customer Support

Round-the-clock customer service and operational support to address your needs anytime. Our dedicated team is always available to ensure your logistics run smoothly.
